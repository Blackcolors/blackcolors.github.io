Blackcolors – “electronic music with a sense of beauty” – blends energetic beats with beautiful landscapes of sounds. His music conveys a fairly human and personal story translating feelings into contrasting sounds and aesthetics.

His new live concept, “Black flute”, acknowledges the organic beauty of live music made with real instruments. His concert flute, analogic keyboard and drum machine take roots in electronic grooves, jazz improvisation and sound design. They invite you to your own journey in his musical world.
